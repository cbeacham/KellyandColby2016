Kelly Findley

Kelly was born and raised in Cincinnati, Ohio. Her parents are Molly and Mark Findley and she has a younger brother Jake. Kelly graduated from Walnut Hills High School where she played Volleyball and Rugby. She then graduated from Purdue University with a degree in Biomedical Engineering and a minor in Psychology. Kelly now works at Procter and Gamble as a Packaging Engineer in Cincinnati, Ohio.

Colby Beacham

Colby was born and raised in Holland, Michigan. His immediate family includes his parents, Chuck and Jami, younger sister, Lily, younger brother, Branden, twin sister, Amber, and her husband, Jesse. Colby went to Black River Public High School and played basketball and soccer. He then went on to attend Purdue University and graduated with a BS in Aeronautical Engineering. Colby is now living in Cincinnati, OH and is an Application Enginneer at Cummins Inc. in Columbus, Indiana.

Our Story

Kelly and Colby met at Purdue University in 2009 during Freshmen Orientation. They started dating when Colby asked Kelly out on Valentines Day, 2010 and have since enjoyed many adventures together. In 2013, Kelly and Colby graduated from Purdue University, moved to Cincinnati, and adopted Mack, their spoiled Pit Bull. In 2014, they took a trip across Europe, visiting Prague, Munich, Paris, Brussels, and Amsterdam. In 2015, Kelly and Colby climbed the Grand Canyon and bought their first home! In 2016, they are looking forward to their wedding followed by a honeymoon in South Africa!
